João Vitor Neves Brandão got his B.Sc. in Physics from Universidade Federal de Viçosa in 2021. In the graduation worked with experimental condensed-matter physics, specifically, studied TbCo thin films produced with electrodeposition, making eletrical and magnetic caracterization, with interest in spintronic applications. This research occurred during 2016-18, with a scholarship from CNPq. During 2018-19, performed a theoretical revision on superconductors, also with CNPq funding.

Participated as coordinator of the 17/18 and 18/19 administrations of the Centro Acadêmico de Física, during the administrations organized two academic weeks.

Nowadays, João is working as a extension technician in the FERMICRO laboratory, as part of the extension project Fermenta Móvel.
